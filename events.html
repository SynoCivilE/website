Recent Events

Synquisitive

SynQuisitive was an event that compelled the students to sit and think about a particular problem statement related to the civil engineering field. The idea was to instill inquisitiveness in the minds of the students about the field and give them a flavour of a real life problem that they might have to face on field.

Autocad and Autodesk Fusion 360 Workshop

A 3-day workshop for AutoCAD and Autodesk Fusion 360 software was conducted as an initiative to prepare the students for both industry and academia. Considering the importance of these software, they become an integral part of the skillset in the resume of a Civil Engineer.

Socha Hai Kya Kabhi

The IR&P council of IITGN had organized a Socha hai kya kabhi series, in which they floated questions worth pondering about but are not related to any specific field. Those are the general problems faced by us in our college or our country. SynOCE provided one such problem statement to the community concerning the advancements in the field of automation.

Treasure Hunt 2.0

Treasure Hunt is yet another online event testing your knowledge about the civil engineering field. The task was to answer the questions given in the PDF document, figure out a code using it and use that code to open the next PDF. This way, the team that solves all the problems in the least amount of time wins. This was the second edition of the event, the first being conducted the previous year.
